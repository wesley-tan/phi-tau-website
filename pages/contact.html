Join Phi Tau!

Thinking about becoming a member? At Phi Tau, our rush system is a bit different. We have rolling rush, which means that you can rush any time, not just in the fall. It's a pretty chill process - at one of our events, just sign the book (it's got a big arrow pointing to it, can't miss it) and show up to a few more of our events in the following couple of weeks, and if we like you, you'll be invited to join the house!
